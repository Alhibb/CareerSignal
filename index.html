Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CareerSignal

How does CareerSignal find relevant jobs?

We use an advanced AI model (Grok) that reads and understands job descriptions like a human. It goes beyond simple keywords to match you based on context, required skills, and career level, ensuring high relevance.

Is CareerSignal really free to start?

Yes! Our Freemium plan is free forever. It's a great way to experience the power of our AI matching with one curated job alert per day. No credit card is required to sign up.

How quickly will I receive job alerts?

Can I customize what types of jobs I receive?

Absolutely. You can set detailed preferences for job titles, required skills, salary range, location (including remote), and experience level. Premium users unlock even more advanced filters.

What makes CareerSignal different from other job alert services?

Three things: relevance (our AI is smarter), speed (instant alerts beat email), and convenience (it all happens in WhatsApp). We focus on quality over quantity to save you time.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your premium subscription at any time with a single click from your account settings. Your premium access will continue until the end of your billing cycle.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and protocols to protect your data. We will never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Do you support international job searches?

Yes, you can set your location preference to anywhere in the world, including fully remote roles. Our AI scans job boards globally to find opportunities that match your criteria.
